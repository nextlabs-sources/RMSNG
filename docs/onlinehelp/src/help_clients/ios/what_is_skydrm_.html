What is SkyDRM?

SkyDRM is a cloud-based service that prevents unauthorized access to files.

SkyDRM converts files to the NextLabs rights-protected format, NXL, and enables you to specify how those files can be accessed or used.

You can define how the files should be protected, whether or not they should have a watermark, when and whether or not the rights protection will expire. The rights protection that can be applied include the rights to view, print, share, or download a file.
